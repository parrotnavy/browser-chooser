Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Browser Chooser so small?

Browser Chooser is built natively with Swift (macOS) and .NET (Windows). This means no bundled Chromium browser, no Node.js runtime‚Äîjust efficient native code. The result? Less 5MB app that starts instantly and uses minimal memory.

Is Browser Chooser free?

Yes. Browser Chooser is free to use. No subscriptions, no tracking, just a better way to browse.

How does it handle my data?

Privacy is our priority. All URL processing and rule evaluation happens locally on your machine. We never collect, store, or transmit your browsing history.

Can I use it on Windows?

Yes! While many URL routers are Mac-only, we offer a first-class native experience for Windows 10 and 11 users.
