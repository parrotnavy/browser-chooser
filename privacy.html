Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Browser Chooser ("Browser Chooser", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website (the "Site") and related downloads.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Site.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

If you contact us (for example, by opening an issue on GitHub), we receive the information you submit such as your message content and any identifiers or profile information associated with the platform you use.

Information collected automatically on the Site

When you visit the Site, we (and our service providers) may automatically collect basic usage information such as pages viewed, approximate location (derived from IP address), device and browser information, and interaction events.

We may use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use the Site. Microsoft Clarity may collect information through cookies or similar technologies. You can learn more and review their privacy information on Microsoft's website.

Hosting and downloads

The Site may be hosted on GitHub Pages and downloads may be served from GitHub. GitHub may collect server logs and other information as described in their privacy documentation.

2. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site and its content.
  • To understand usage trends and improve performance and usability.
  • To respond to questions, feedback, and support requests.
  • To protect the security and integrity of the Site.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the Site (for example, analytics providers).
  • With third-party platforms you choose to use (for example, GitHub when you open an issue).
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
  • To protect rights, safety, and security (for example, to prevent abuse).

4. Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Retention periods can vary depending on the type of data and where it is processed (for example, on third-party platforms such as GitHub or Microsoft Clarity).

5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your choices

  • You can limit or block cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can use browser extensions or settings to block analytics scripts.
  • If you contact us via a third-party platform (such as GitHub), you can manage or delete that content subject to the platform's features and policies.

7. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was most recently revised.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub issues: https://github.com/parrotnavy/browser-chooser/issues.
